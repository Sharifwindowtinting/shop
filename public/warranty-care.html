Warranty & Care

Care instructions can vary by film, coating, vehicle, and installation scope. Always follow the specific guidance provided at pickup.

Window Tint Care

  • Do not roll windows down until the installer-approved curing period has passed.
  • Use a soft microfiber towel and ammonia-free glass cleaner.
  • Small moisture pockets can be normal during curing and should clear as the film dries.

Paint Protection Film Care

  • Avoid pressure washing film edges during the initial curing period.
  • Wash gently with pH-neutral soap and clean microfiber towels.
  • Contact the shop before polishing, compounding, or using aggressive chemicals on protected panels.
